# 🇪🇬 Exploring Egypt's Economic Evolution (1991 - 2023)

## 📌 Project Overview
This is a comprehensive macro-economic analysis of Egypt's journey over three decades. Using data from the **World Bank**, I analyzed key indicators such as GDP/GNP growth, trade deficits, and demographic impacts to understand the challenges and milestones of the Egyptian economy.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Process
* **Data Sourcing:** Extracted 30+ years of economic indicators from the **World Bank Open Data**.
* **Data Cleaning & Analysis:** Processed and normalized the data using **Excel** to ensure consistency across decades.
* **Visualization:** Built an interactive dashboard to correlate population growth with economic pressure.
* **Content Strategy:** Authored a detailed analytical article to explain the "Story behind the numbers."

---

## 🚀 Strategic Insights (Highlights)

### 💰 GDP & Trade Balance
* **The GNP Leap:** Egypt's GNP grew from **$37.4B (1991)** to a peak of **$476.7B (2022)**, showing massive expansion in economic capacity.
* **Trade Deficit Challenge:** The deficit widened significantly, reaching **$18B in 2023**, highlighting the urgent need for export diversification.

### 👥 Population & Employment
* **The Demographic Pressure:** Population surged from **58.6M to 112.9M**, acting as both a driver for domestic demand and a strain on resources.
* **Labor Market:** Unemployment dropped from **13.2% (2013) to 7.3% (2023)**, though youth unemployment remains a critical focus area.

### 💹 Inflation & Foreign Investment
* **Inflation Impact:** 2023 saw inflation hit **33.9%**, significantly affecting the real value of the average salary increase.
* **Investment Peak:** Foreign investment reached an all-time high of **$34B in 2022**, a crucial pillar for infrastructure development.
